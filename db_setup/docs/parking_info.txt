GreenPark Central is a modern urban parking facility designed to support both short-term visitors and long-term commuters in the downtown district. The parking complex combines indoor and outdoor spaces and uses automated entry and exit systems based on license-plate recognition and mobile booking confirmation. The goal of the service is to reduce congestion around the city center by allowing drivers to reserve spaces in advance and access clear information about availability, pricing, and operating rules.

The facility is located at 14 Riverside Avenue in the central business area, within walking distance of Central Station, River Mall, and the City Library. Drivers arriving from Highway M3 can access the parking area via Exit 12, which leads directly toward Riverside Avenue. Entry barriers are positioned on Riverside Avenue and North Street, while exits are automated and open once payment or an active booking is verified. Clear signage and digital navigation screens help drivers find available spaces and locate elevators or pedestrian exits.

GreenPark Central operates continuously throughout the year and remains open twenty-four hours a day, including public holidays. Although access to the parking area is always available, customer support services operate between 06:00 and 22:00. Overnight parking is permitted as long as a valid booking exists or the vehicle has an active session. Security is maintained through camera monitoring and periodic staff inspections to ensure a safe environment for visitors.

The parking structure accommodates approximately 420 vehicles and includes designated areas for electric vehicles, accessible parking spaces near elevators, and covered premium zones for customers seeking additional protection from weather conditions. The maximum allowed vehicle height is 2.1 meters, and all vehicles must be parked within marked spaces. Charging stations for electric vehicles are located on the ground level and require both a parking session and a charging fee.

Bookings are handled primarily through the mobile application or website. Users select an arrival time, view available zones, and confirm a reservation before arriving at the facility. Once a booking is confirmed, the system associates the reservation with either a QR code or the vehicle’s license plate number. When the vehicle arrives, the barrier opens automatically if the reservation is valid. Drivers can extend their stay remotely through the app if capacity allows, and digital receipts are generated automatically after the session ends.

Pricing varies depending on the selected parking zone and duration of stay. Standard spaces are charged at a moderate hourly rate with a daily maximum limit, while premium covered spaces carry a slightly higher fee due to their location and protection. Electric vehicle charging is billed separately based on energy consumption. Short grace periods are offered for quick stops, and reduced weekend rates are available to encourage off-peak usage.

Customers may cancel or modify their reservations directly within the application. Cancellations made shortly before the scheduled arrival are free of charge, while later cancellations may incur a partial fee. The system is designed to provide flexibility, allowing drivers to update their bookings or extend their parking session without returning to the vehicle.

To maintain safety and fair usage, certain rules apply within the facility. Hazardous materials are not permitted, vehicles must remain within designated spaces, and overnight camping inside vehicles is prohibited. In cases where a ticket or booking confirmation cannot be located, a service fee may apply to validate exit.
